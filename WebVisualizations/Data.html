Percent of applicants who identify as women was calculated by dividing the number of women applicants by the total number of applicants, and multiplying that by 100 (for each University and each year). For the figure on the home page, for each University and each year evaluated, I used the number of applicants that identify as women divided by total number of admitted applicants, and multiplied that number by 100.
